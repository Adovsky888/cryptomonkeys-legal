Privacy Policy — CryptoMonkeys Uploader

Last updated: 13 June 2026

CryptoMonkeys Uploader (the “App”) is an internal content-publishing tool operated by the CryptoMonkeys team (the “Operator”). The App is used by the Operator to publish the Operator’s own short videos to the Operator’s own accounts on TikTok, YouTube and Instagram through those platforms’ official APIs.

What data the App uses

  • Authorization tokens. When the Operator connects a social account, the platform issues an OAuth access/refresh token. The App uses these tokens only to upload the Operator’s own videos and related metadata (title, description, tags) to the Operator’s own account.
  • Video content and metadata created by the Operator, for the sole purpose of publishing it to the connected accounts.

What the App does NOT do

  • It does not collect, store, sell or share personal data of any third parties or viewers.
  • It does not access, read or store other users’ private data, followers, or messages.
  • It does not use the data for advertising or profiling.

Storage and security

Authorization tokens are stored privately on the Operator’s own machine (in a local environment file that is not shared or committed to source control) and are used only to call the platform APIs. Tokens can be revoked at any time from the respective platform’s settings.

Data retention

Tokens are kept until the Operator revokes access or deletes them. Published videos live on the respective platforms under the platforms’ own policies.

Third-party platforms

Publishing happens via TikTok, YouTube (Google) and Instagram (Meta). Their handling of the published content is governed by their own privacy policies.
